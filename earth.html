Earth - The Blue Planet

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to harbor life. It is 70% covered in water and has a diverse range of climates and ecosystems.

Key Facts about Earth:

  • Type: Terrestrial Planet
  • Distance from the Sun: 149.6 million kilometers
  • Diameter: 12,742 kilometers
  • Surface Temperature: Average 15°C (59°F)
  • Atmosphere: 78% nitrogen, 21% oxygen
  • Moons: 1 (The Moon)

Earth's atmosphere protects life by blocking harmful solar radiation and moderating temperatures, while its gravity interacts with the Sun and the Moon to create seasons and tides.
