Neptune - The Blue Planet

Neptune is the eighth and farthest planet from the Sun in the Solar System. It is known for its striking blue color and intense storms.

Key Facts about Neptune:

  • Type: Ice Giant
  • Distance from the Sun: 4.5 billion kilometers
  • Diameter: 49,244 kilometers
  • Surface Temperature: -214°C (-353°F)
  • Atmosphere: Hydrogen, helium, and methane
  • Moons: 14 known moons, including Triton

Neptune has the fastest winds in the Solar System, reaching speeds of over 2,000 kilometers per hour. It also has a faint ring system.
