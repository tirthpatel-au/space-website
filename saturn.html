Saturn - The Ringed Planet

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and is known for its extensive ring system. It is a gas giant composed mainly of hydrogen and helium.

Key Facts about Saturn:

  • Type: Gas Giant
  • Distance from the Sun: 1.4 billion kilometers
  • Diameter: 116,460 kilometers
  • Surface Temperature: -139°C (-218°F)
  • Atmosphere: Mainly hydrogen and helium
  • Moons: 82 confirmed moons, including Titan

Saturn's rings are made up of ice and rock particles, and its largest moon, Titan, has a dense atmosphere and is larger than the planet Mercury.
