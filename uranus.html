Uranus - The Ice Giant

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun and is unique in that it rotates on its side, making it appear to roll around the Sun rather than spin like other planets.

Key Facts about Uranus:

  • Type: Ice Giant
  • Distance from the Sun: 2.87 billion kilometers
  • Diameter: 50,724 kilometers
  • Surface Temperature: -224°C (-371°F)
  • Atmosphere: Hydrogen, helium, and methane
  • Moons: 27 confirmed moons

Uranus has a faint ring system and is the coldest planet in the Solar System. The methane in its atmosphere gives it a blue-green color.
